07 · How it works

Simple steps.
No runaround.

You call or send a message, we look at the space, agree on scope and price, then GM Carpentry installs. Most single-room trim jobs are a few days to a couple of weeks depending on detail.

  1. i. Step 1

    Call or
    message

    Tell us what room, what you want installed, and your timeline. Photos help — we'll follow up quickly.

  2. ii. Step 2

    Site visit
    & quote

    GM Carpentry measures on site, confirms profiles and materials, and gives you a clear written quote before work starts.

  3. iii. Step 3

    Prep &
    materials

    Stock is ordered and cut for your job. Built-up crown and custom details are assembled before install day.

  4. iv. Step 4

    Install &
    walkthrough

08 · Materials

Quality wood
that holds up.

For trim that gets bumped, painted and lived with, solid wood and quality primed stock outperform cheap MDF every time. We'll recommend the right material for your room and budget — no upsell, just what fits the job.
