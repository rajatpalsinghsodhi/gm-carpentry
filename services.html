Specified hardwood,
by application.

Each species is chosen for a reason. The most common we mill and the application we usually mill it for.

White Oak

Cathedral grain that fumes beautifully. Cabinetry, floors, exposed beams.

Black Walnut

Rich chocolate with violet undertones. Libraries, studies, fine cabinetry.
